Relient K

About the band

Relient K is a band that formed in 1998 in Ohio. Although their primary genre of music is rock, they have dabbled into Christian rock, alternative, punk, and pop. The two main members of the band are Matt Hoopes and Matt Thiessen. Through the years there have been many other members alongside the two Matts.

Discography

The band's debut album was released in 2000, with 10 subsequent albums spanning the years until their most recent album was released in 2016.

  1. Relient K (2000)
  2. The Anatomy of the Tongue in Cheek (2001)
  3. Two Lefts Don't Make a Right... but Three Do (2003)
  4. Deck the Halls, Bruise Your Hand (2003)
  5. Mmhmm (2004)
  6. Five Score and Seven Years Ago (2007)
  7. Let it Snow, Baby... Let it Reindeer (2007)
  8. Forget and Not Slow Down (2009)
  9. Is for Karaoke (2011)
  10. Collapsible Lung (2013)
  11. Air for Free (2016)

Personal Reasons

I have loved Relient K since I was a pre-teen. I have seen them live many times. They were my first live concert I attended in St. Pete, FL. I like that their lyrics have a lot of depth and substance, but that they have a humorous quality, as well. I like the personalities of the two main band members. I would love for them to release more music. They recently announced that they are going back on tour after a long hiatus, which makes me hopeful that they make release new music soon. Their long career is truly admirable. Not very many bands can last 20+ years and still maintain a dedicated fan base like they have.
